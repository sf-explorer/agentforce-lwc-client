# Received Leads Today

## Prompt

Objective: Provide a clear daily lead intake snapshot.

Instructions:

1. Retrieve all leads created or received today.
2. Display each lead with source, creation time, owner, status, and priority.
3. Sort by priority first, then creation time (newest first).
4. Add a short summary: total leads and leads requiring immediate follow-up.

Output format:

- Prioritized lead list
- Total leads today
- Immediate follow-up count

## Expected Result

A prioritized list of today's leads is displayed with a clear summary of total volume and urgent follow-ups.
