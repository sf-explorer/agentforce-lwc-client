# Client Portfolio Analysis

## Prompt

Objective: Identify high-value cross-sell opportunities across the client portfolio.

Instructions:

1. Analyze each customer's current contracts.
2. Detect missing product lines (home, auto, health, protection, savings).
3. Rank the top 10 opportunities by estimated revenue potential.
4. For each opportunity, provide:

- Why this is a strong opportunity
- A personalized commercial approach

5. Use CRM Assistant sub-agent and email the final report to the manager.

Output format:

- Top 10 ranked opportunities
- Missing products by customer
- Personalized outreach strategy per opportunity

## Expected Result

A ranked Top 10 cross-sell report is generated with revenue potential, product gap analysis, and personalized sales approaches, then sent by email.
