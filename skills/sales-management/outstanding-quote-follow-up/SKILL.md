# Outstanding Quote Follow-up

## Instructions

Objective: Increase conversion of quotes pending customer response.

Scope: Quotes sent more than 5 days ago with no customer reply.

Instructions:
1) Retrieve all eligible quotes.
2) Draft a personalized follow-up email per quote that:
- Recaps the offer
- Highlights key coverage strengths
- Proposes availability for Q&A
3) Send emails immediately via CRM Assistant (no approval required).
4) Create a follow-up call task due in 3 days for quotes above €2,000.
5) Send a recap of all follow-ups performed.

Output format:
- Quotes followed up
- Emails sent
- Call tasks created (> €2,000)
- Follow-up recap
