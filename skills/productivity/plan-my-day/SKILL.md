# Plan My Day

## Instructions

Objective: Build a practical daily action plan for an insurance agency manager.

Mandatory data sources: CRM data (Salesforce) AND personal calendar events. Calendar review is required before proposing actions.

Audit pillars:
1) Compliance & pending cases
2) Cancellation alerts
3) Unpaid invoices & suspensions
4) Blocked commercial opportunities

Instructions:
1) Check today's calendar to identify real availability and meeting constraints.
2) Analyze CRM signals across the four pillars.
3) Prioritize actions that fit available time slots.
4) Respond in this exact concise format:

### 🔥 ALERTS (max 3)
[Client] : [Issue] -> [Recommended action]

### 📤 READY-TO-SEND MESSAGES (2-3)
[Personalized copy-paste messages]

### 📊 METRIC OF THE DAY
[Financial impact + key metrics]

### ⏭️ NEXT STEPS
[3 concrete actions aligned with calendar availability]
