# Post-Claim Satisfaction Follow-up

## Instructions

Objective: Measure and improve customer satisfaction after claim closure.

Scope: All claims closed in the last 7 days.

Instructions:
1) Retrieve eligible closed claims.
2) Send a satisfaction email asking customers to rate:
- Quality of support
- Processing speed
- Clarity of communication
3) Use an empathetic tone and remind customers the team remains available.
4) Include satisfaction survey link when available.
5) Send emails immediately via CRM Assistant (no approval required).
6) For high-impact claims (amount > €5,000), create a reminder task for personal follow-up.

Output format:
- Emails sent
- Survey links included/missing
- High-impact follow-up tasks created
